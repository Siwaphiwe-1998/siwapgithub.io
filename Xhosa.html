Ngena

Chwetheza iinkcukacha ozigcinileyo ukuze ungene. Faka igama lakho lomsebenzisi kwibhokisi yokuqala yokubhaliweyo kwaye ufake ipassword yakho kwibhokisi yesibini yokubhaliweyo, ukuba awukwazi ukungena ngemvume nokuba uneakhawunti, nceda unxibelelane neenkonzo zabathengi. ukuba umtsha kwaye awunayo iakhawunti, cofa indawo enerejista esezantsi ukuze ubhalise iakhawunti entsha, xa ugqibile, cofa kwindawo yokugcina ukugcina iinkcukacha zakho kwaye ubhalise iakhawunti yakho

Bhala isikhalazo

xa ubhala isikhalazo sakho ungalibali ukugcwalisa zonke iibhokisi ezibhaliweyo ngolwazi lwakho, qiniseka ukuba ugcwalisa idilesi efanelekileyo kunye ne-imeyile, ukuze sikwazi ukuqhakamshelana nawe ukuze sikhalimele ukuba siye kule ngxaki kwindawo yakho. Ukuzalisa idilesi echanekileyo kunye nenombolo yoqhakamshelwano kuyakwenza ukuba inkqubo ikhawuleze ukugqitywa

Ngenisa iSikhalazo

Xa ugqibile ukubhala isikhalazo sakho, sicebisa ukuba uthathe ixesha elithile ukujonga kwakhona iinkcukacha ozifakileyo ukuqinisekisa ukuba zichanekile. Xa ugqibile ngaloo nto, cofa kwiqhosha eliluhlaza lokungenisa isikhalazo sakho.
