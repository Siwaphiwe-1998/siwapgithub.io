how to check for water and electricty bill

  • click on bill
  • chose between the two otions
  • click calculate

click on bill

when you click on bill you will be presented with two options, water and electricity, the bill tab shows you two submenu where you can chose one or the other in order to get the total bill for the option you have chosen in the submenus

chose between the two otions

when you have clicked one of the options in tab "Bill", you will be taken to a form where yoou will enter the meter for the water or electriciy, depending on the option you have chosen. make sure to enter the correct meter on the text box in order to avoid incorrect charges

Click Calculate

when you are done filling in the text field with the water or electricity meter,click on the button labbelled "Calculate" below, and the total amount will be displayed on the last text field.

Read the paragragh to learn more about Mnquma Local municipality

Mnquma Local Municipality is located in the South Eastern part of the Eastern Cape Province. This category B municipality falls under the jurisdiction of the Amathole District Municipality (ADM) and comprises of an amalgamation of the former Butterworth, Ngqamakhwe and Centane TRC’s.Mnquma Municipaliy shares borders with three other local municipalities i.e. Mbhashe, Intsika Yethu and Great Kei Municipality. This also includes a number of previously administered rural areas. It is approximately 32 995 240 square kilometres and consists of 31 wards. Mnquma Local Municipality has a total population of approximately 252 390 people, 99% of which are Xhosa speaking African. The remaining 1% of the population includes English, Afrikaans, Zulu and Sesotho speaking people. The municipality comprises of 54% female and 46% male of the total population and consists of approximately 75410 households.
